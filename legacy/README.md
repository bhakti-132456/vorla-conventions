# Vorla Conventions Website - Complete Redesign

## Overview
A premium, modern, multi-page website for Vorla Conventions - AC Function Halls & Outdoor Lawns in Hyderabad. The website showcases the venue's facilities, events, and services with a sophisticated design and SEO optimization.

### Pages Created:
1. **index.html** - Homepage

   - Why choose Vorla Conventions

   - Events preview
   - Legacy teaser
   - Call-to-action sections

2. **about.html** - About Page
   - Company story and legacy
   - Journey timeline (2008-2025)

   - Outdoor Party Lawns information

   - Engagements & Pre-wedding Functions
   - Birthdays & Family Celebrations
   - Corporate Events & Conferences

   - Cultural Programs

   - Tabbed gallery (All Photos, Venue Exteriors, Interior Spaces, Event Setups)
   - All 5 real venue images organized by category
   - Hover effects and captions

6. **contact.html** - Contact & Location
   - Contact information cards (Phone, WhatsApp, Email, Location)
   - Detailed inquiry form
   - Google Maps integration
   - FAQ section (8 questions)
   - Multiple CTAs
